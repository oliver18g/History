Jeff B
Beach; Scenic Reserve - all stunning.

On our way to Gisborne we went first to the Mahia Penisula Scenic Reserve and did a 4.5 km. track. Long distance views across mountains and water were interspersed with forest that felt like Jurassic Park. It was lovely. Finished the day with a visit to Mahia Beach for a quick swim. Great place to spend time.

Wayne N
Wild open area

About 90km south of Gisborne the Mahia Peninsula is a 20km detour off the main road heading to Napier. The colour of the ocean and stark beach fronts are spectacular. At Mahia Beach there is camping and accommodation which appears popular. Also public toilets. Pub and shops. We loved this detour and recommend it, if you are in no hurry.

Mahia, one of the best holiday spots to visit in the North Island. Whether you just want to relax on the beach and sunbathe, go on a fishing trip out deep or go surfing in some of New Zealands best surf spots theres always something for you.
